Table 1: Univariates

We want:

  • gender (male)
  • birthweight (brthwk.p.i)
  • ethnicity (srh.a.i)
  • race (racehp2p)
  • PEDS risk categories (peds)
  • age (srage.p)
  • uninsured in the last year (unins.ever)
  • Developmental specialist referred (cf46)
  • speech referred (cf47)
  • Referred to either (referred)
